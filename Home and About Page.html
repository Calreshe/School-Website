ABOUT US

The public High School is located within the Central Karoo District in Prince Albert , South Africa. We accomodate an average of 600 learners each year ranging from grade 8 to 12. Our goal is to provide our learners with the best education and life skills to best face the world that awaits them.Our mission at Zwartberg High School is to empower our learners and instill in them a creative mindset and a passion for learning something new each day. we provide a number of support systems to achieve our mission, to better our learners education and urge them to set goal for themselves with a plan to achieve it. a motto we love to use is, any dream can come true, if you work hard enough to achieve it.
